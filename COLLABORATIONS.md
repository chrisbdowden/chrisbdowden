# Collaboration Opportunities

I'm actively seeking partnerships with computational biology labs and research institutions exploring biomarker discovery, feature selection methodologies, and reproducible ML pipelines.

---

## What We Can Offer

### BPF Validation on Your Cohorts
We can apply the Biomarker Probability Fusion methodology to your existing datasets at **no cost** in exchange for co-authorship on resulting publications.

**What you provide:**
- Cohort data (expression, genomic, imaging, or multi-modal)
- Clinical labels (outcome/phenotype)
- Access guidance (if restricted-access data)

**What we provide:**
- Complete BPF analysis pipeline
- Full preprocessing & QC
- Bootstrap confidence intervals + permutation testing
- Interpretation + validation strategy
- Manuscript draft (you're lead author, we're co-author)

**Timeline:** 4–8 weeks depending on data complexity  
**Output:** Co-authored publication (target journal: your choice)

---

### Multi-Modal Biomarker Fusion
If you're working with **multi-omic datasets** (genomics + transcriptomics + proteomics + imaging), we can design custom fusion strategies:

- **Weighted fusion** (each modality ranked independently, then combined)
- **Modality-specific thresholds** (adaptive AUC cutoffs per data type)
- **Cross-modal validation** (stability across omics layers)

**Ideal for:** 
- Cancer precision medicine (genomic + imaging risk stratification)
- Neurodegenerative disease (fluid biomarkers + neuroimaging)
- Rare disease phenotyping (sparse data + multiple modality types)

---

### Reproducibility Auditing (CBAE Framework)
Your ML pipeline can be evaluated against the **18-assertion CBAE framework** for:

- Information barrier integrity (no hyperparameter leakage)
- Deterministic reproducibility (seed governance)
- Stability under noise
- Bounded deployment envelope (what data falls within your model's validity?)

**Output:** CBAE compliance report + recommendations for pipeline hardening

---

### Prospective Validation Studies
If you have **prodromal biomarkers** or **early detection signals**, we can help design prospective validation protocols:

- Statistical power calculations
- Sample size estimation
- Blinded validation strategies
- Regulatory readiness assessment (FDA guidance prep)

**Relevant for:** Early PD detection, early AD detection, cancer screening in high-risk populations

## Collaboration Models

### Model 1: Co-Author Partnership (Standard)
- **Investment from you:** Data + clinical expertise
- **Investment from us:** Analysis + manuscript development
- **Outcome:** Joint publication (you first author, we co-author)
- **Timeline:** 4–8 weeks
- **Cost:** $0 (academic partnership)

**Best for:** Validating BPF on a new disease or cohort you care deeply about

---

### Model 2: Research Collaboration Agreement (Formal)
- **Structured partnership** with formal agreement
- **Defined IP ownership** (typically joint)
- **Scope:** Multi-paper effort (2–3 joint publications planned)
- **Timeline:** 6–12 months
- **Cost:** Variable (can discuss research support)

**Best for:** Sustained partnership exploring multiple research questions

---

### Model 3: Service Agreement (Fee-Based)
For organizations with **immediate needs** (grant deadlines, clinical validation, etc.):

- **Analysis-only option** (you provide data, we perform BPF, you interpret)
- **Consulting option** (methodology guidance for your team)
- **Reproducibility audit** (CBAE compliance assessment)

**Cost:** Negotiable based on scope  
**Timeline:** 2–4 weeks

**Best for:** Pharmaceutical companies, clinical research organizations, biotech

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Do you require exclusive access to our data?
**A:** No. BPF results are fully reproducible from your published data. We only need access for analysis; you retain all IP rights.

### Q: What if we want to keep biomarker results proprietary?
**A:** We can work under confidentiality agreements. Methodology papers are separate from application papers—you can publish one without the other.

### Q: How do you handle authorship on multi-lab collaborations?
**A:** ICMJE guidelines. Typically: your lab (lead author), our lab (methods author + co-author). Negotiable based on contribution.

### Q: What if Bioinformatics or GigaScience accepts your papers during our collaboration?
**A:** Great news! Your independent validation becomes a separate, confirmatory publication. Published methodology + your validation = stronger story.

### Q: Do you charge for validation work?
**A:** For academic partners: no (co-authorship model). For industry partners: yes (consulting rates negotiable).

### Q: Can BPF handle my specific data type? (single-cell, spatial, imaging, etc.)
**A:** Core methodology is univariate AUC ranking + adaptive selection. We've validated on:
- ✅ RNA-seq (bulk and ADNI microarray)
- ✅ Genomics (SNP arrays, whole genome)
- ✅ DNA methylation (Illumina EPIC)
- ✅ Multi-modal omics (fusion)
- ⚠️ Single-cell (theoretically yes, limited validation)
- ⚠️ Imaging (in early exploration)
