Your ML pipeline can be evaluated against the **18-assertion CBAE reproducibility framework** for:

- Stability under noise
- Bounded deployment envelope

**Output:** CBAE compliance report + recommendations

## FAQ

### Q: Do you require exclusive data access?
**A:** No. BPF results are fully reproducible. You retain all IP rights.

### Q: Can BPF handle my data type?
**A:** Core methodology is univariate AUC ranking + adaptive selection. Validated on:
- ✅ RNA-seq (bulk + microarray)
- ✅ Genomics (SNP arrays, whole genome)
- ✅ DNA methylation (Illumina EPIC)
- ✅ Multi-modal omics (fusion)
- ⚠️ Single-cell (theoretically yes, limited validation)
- ⚠️ Imaging (early exploration)

### Q: What if your papers get accepted during our collaboration?
**A:** Great news! Your independent validation becomes a separate confirmatory publication. Published methodology + your validation = stronger story.
